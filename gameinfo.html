Game Information

This is a choose your own story game which allows the user to choose which path they would like the story to go. This game follows the journey of princess Freya returning home to find out that a spell has been cast on her family by a witch. Freya will travel the land on her quest and collect items to break the spell and save her family. Freya will meet some dangerous characters on the way and will have some decisions to make that could affect the quest and could lead to her death.

This game creation was inspired by my daughters storylines and imagination.
