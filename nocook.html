No time for cooking

You decide that you should wait till you get closer to home before cooking incase you encounter any problems on the way. So the trio and their new member start to pack up and march towards Lemorphia.On the way word spreads and other strangers start to appear. "Burn the witch" could be heard as well as "She is evil" as new member start to join the group. . But as word spreads, the witch also knows of your presence and gathers her minions to attack. You and allies fight bravely and are victorious but the attack was a decoy so the witch could cast a spell turning everyone to stone. GAME OVER
